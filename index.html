What is I'm Not a Human?

At its core, I'm Not a Human is a first-person psychological horror game. Your simple but cruel premise: the sun scorches the world by day, and at night, “Visitors” that mimic humans roam. You live in a small house, and people knock, begging to come in. Your only goal is to survive by correctly identifying and eliminating the fakes, or "Visitors." This game has no run-and-gun action; the tension comes from interpreting imperfect signals and living with the consequences of every choice.

How to Play I'm Not a Human: The Core Loop

Each night brings a new visitor to your door. Your task is to examine them using your limited energy budget. You'll check their physical tells—like their teeth, nails, or eyes—and their behavioral tells, such as their word choice or inconsistencies in their story. News broadcasts on the TV introduce new clues as the days pass. Your decisions are not mechanical—it’s a blend of intuition and data.
