I'm Not a Human: The Definitive Guide to All Visitors and Their Tells

  In I'm Not a Human, survival hinges on one thing: your ability to distinguish real humans from terrifying fakes, or "Visitors." Every visitor you encounter has unique tells—subtle clues that betray their true nature. This guide is your ultimate resource, breaking down the physical and behavioral signs you should never ignore to survive every night and unlock all endings.

The Golden Rule: Don't Trust Anyone

  • No Obvious Signs: Real humans can sometimes exhibit tells, and fakes may not show any.
  • Energy Management: You have limited energy to perform inspections. Use it wisely.
  • Information is Power: Pay close attention to TV news broadcasts and clues from your environment.
  • The Consequence: A wrong decision can lead to your demise or a different ending.

All Visitors: The Tells You Can't Ignore

Physical and Behavioral Tells

  1. **The Mushroom Visitor:** Keep an eye out for a specific pattern of fungi on their skin. This tell is a dead giveaway, but only after a certain day in the game.
  2. **The Coat Guy:** This visitor is known for their distinctive coat, but the real tell is what you can't see. Pay close attention to their hands and the subtle sounds they make.
  3. **The Tall Man:** He might seem normal, but his unusual height is only the first clue. Check for inconsistencies in his story and the way he reacts to your questions.
  4. **The Aura:** Some fakes have a faint, unsettling aura. While difficult to spot, it's one of the most reliable tells. You'll need to use your energy to specifically check for this.
  5. **The Beggar:** This visitor will plead for entry. Their desperation might be a tell in itself, but focus on the details of their story and any contradictions.

How to Spot a Fake: My Strategy

  1. **Initial Scan:** Listen to their voice and story first. Fakes often have odd pauses or strange word choices.
  2. **Use Your Energy:** Prioritize high-value inspections like teeth and eyes. Don’t waste energy on low-yield checks unless you have a surplus.
  3. **Context is Key:** Always cross-reference what a visitor says with the information from the TV news.

FAQs & Community Insights

Can I survive without killing anyone?

The game’s core mechanics often force difficult choices. While it might be possible, it is incredibly challenging to avoid all conflict.

What's the difference between I'm Not a Human and I'm Not Human?

They are the same game. The title is often shortened in player searches and discussions.

Is there an official I'm Not a Human wiki?

While there isn't an official wiki, our site serves as a comprehensive hub for all things related to the game, including detailed information on all visitors and endings.
