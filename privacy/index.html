PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website, particularly our guides and resources for **I'm Not a Human**, including the **I'm Not a Human wiki**, **all visitors** guides, and **all endings** walkthroughs.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We may collect certain types of information to improve your experience and provide relevant content. This data is collected in a way that respects your privacy and is not tied to your personal identity.

Non-Personal Information:

  • This includes data about how you use our site, such as which pages you visit, the time you spend on them, and the links you click. This helps us understand what content is most helpful.
  • We may also collect anonymized data about your device and browser, such as the operating system and screen size, to ensure our site works well on all devices.

Usage Data:

  • We collect general data on user behavior to analyze trends. For example, we might track how many people visit our **no i'm not a human wiki** or which of our guides on **how to play I'm Not a Human** gets the most traffic.
  • This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Cookies and Similar Technologies:

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and provide a consistent browsing experience. For example, we might use cookies to remember if you've already seen a pop-up.
  • You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

The information we collect is used to:

Improve Our Content:

  • To analyze which **I'm Not a Human** guides, like the one for **all endings**, are most popular and to create more content you find valuable.
  • To understand user behavior and optimize our website's structure and layout for better navigation.

Enhance User Experience:

  • To personalize your experience by showing you content that is most relevant to your interests.

Security and Analytics:

  • To monitor website traffic and ensure the site's security and stability.
  • To perform statistical analysis to better understand our audience.

3. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not share any personal information. The non-personal, aggregated data we collect may be shared with:

Third-Party Analytics Providers:

  • We may use services like Google Analytics to help us understand website traffic and usage patterns. This data is anonymized.

Legal Requirements:

  • We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a valid legal process.

Business Transfers:

  • In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, your anonymized data may be transferred to the new entity.

4. DATA SECURITY

We are committed to protecting your data. We use industry-standard security measures to ensure your information is safe.

Security Measures:

  • We use SSL encryption to secure data transmitted between your browser and our website.
  • Our servers are protected by firewalls and other security protocols to prevent unauthorized access.

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain non-personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no need to retain it.

Usage Data:

  • We retain anonymized usage data for analytical purposes to improve our website.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our website is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Parental Consent:

  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Since we do not collect personal information, there are no personal data rights to exercise. You can, however, control your browsing experience.

Cookie Controls:

  • You have the right to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.

8. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Notification:

  • We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
